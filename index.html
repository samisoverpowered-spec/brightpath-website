The Pomelo English Method

A structured curriculum,
built for results.

Unlike general tutoring, Pomelo English follows a purpose-built curriculum designed to move students through clear, measurable stages — from foundational skills to confident, independent English use.

01

Language Foundations

Grammar, sentence structure, and vocabulary built systematically — ensuring every student has a solid base before advancing.

02

Academic Writing

Essay structure, argumentation, and academic style — the skills that directly impact grades in school and university.

03

Reading & Analysis

Critical reading, comprehension strategies, and analytical thinking — so students can engage with complex texts confidently.

04

Oral Communication

Pronunciation, spoken fluency, and presentation skills — building the confidence to speak clearly in any situation.

05

Exam Preparation

Targeted IELTS and TOEFL strategies, timed practice, and examiner-aware feedback — built into the curriculum, not bolted on.

06

Applied & Professional English

Business writing, professional communication, and real-world English — for students preparing to enter the workforce.

The Pomelo English curriculum is actively being developed and refined. Each student's programme is personalised to their level and goals within this framework.
